About

Engineer and designer,
equally at home in CAD
and a sketchbook.

I'm a fourth-year Mechanical Engineering and Design student at Northeastern University, graduating December 2026. My work lives at the intersection of physical product development and thoughtful design — I care about how things are made as much as how they look and feel.

I've had the chance to work at Roam, a consumer travel startup in Brooklyn where I contributed to a patent-pending design, at Ideal Tridon on manufacturing engineering, and as a freelance CAD consultant for Framebridge.

Outside of work, I make things: mechanical keyboards, sewing projects, music, charcoal drawings. Making is making.
