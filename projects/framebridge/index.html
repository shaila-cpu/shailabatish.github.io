Framebridge

Role Freelance Design Consultant
Timeline Dec 2023 – Mar 2024
Deliverable 15+ SolidWorks CAD models
Status In active production

Overview

Framebridge is a custom framing company known for its range of frame styles sold across digital and physical retail. I was brought on as a freelance design consultant to expand their product catalog — designing new frame profiles and customization variants in SolidWorks.

The work required balancing creative design latitude with the tight constraints of manufacturable profiles: wall thickness, joinery tolerances, and the visual language consistent with the existing Framebridge line.

All 15+ models I designed are now in active production across Framebridge's digital and physical retail channels.

Process

Each frame profile began with a brief from the Framebridge team defining target aesthetics, price point, and any dimensional constraints. I developed models in SolidWorks using extrude, sweep, and loft operations to build accurate cross-section profiles, then assembled full frame corners to verify miter fit and visual coherence.

Designs went through review cycles with the client before being finalized and handed off as production-ready CAD files. Working remotely required clear documentation and clean file organization throughout.

What I Learned

This engagement pushed my SolidWorks skills significantly — particularly around maintaining parametric flexibility in models so that a single profile could be resized across multiple aspect ratios without rebuilding geometry. It also gave me real experience working within an established brand's design system and understanding how aesthetic decisions interact with manufacturing constraints.
